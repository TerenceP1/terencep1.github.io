How accurate is this?

This calculator should be very accurate as it follows the official USCF rating calculation paper HERE The bonus factor will be updated to the best of my ability.

What info do I need?

You may need lots of info. If the player or you are unrated, you need to ask if they have FIDE then CFC then their exact birthday. This is one of the few strange special cases. You also need the tounament clock settings and tournament date. If the tournament is multi-day, speak with a tournament director or staff.
